ABOUT US

At Blue Diamond Detailing, we believe every car deserves to shine like new. Based in Utah County, we’re a passionate auto detailing service dedicated to delivering top-tier results with unmatched convenience and care.

Founded in 2023, Blue Diamond Detailing started with a simple idea: make professional-grade car care easy, accessible, and affordable for everyone. What began as a weekend side hustle has grown into a trusted detailing service known for precision, professionalism, and honest service.

Why chose us?

We come to you - Home or office, we detail on your schedule.

Detail-focused – No shortcuts. Every inch gets the attention it deserves.

Eco-conscious – We use safe, high-quality, and environmentally friendly products.

Cheap prices – Professional results without breaking the bank.



From daily drivers to show cars, we treat every vehicle like it’s our own. Whether you’re looking for a quick clean-up or a full interior and exterior transformation, Blue Diamond Detailing is here to make your car look and feel its best.
